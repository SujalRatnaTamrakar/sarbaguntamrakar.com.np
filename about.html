Himalayan Traditional

Why Himalayan?

Himalayan Traditional Craft is a private professional group organized to support village and other low income producer with appropriate design market and technical skill needed for labour intensive production of traditional craft in Nepal.

What we offer

Himalayan traditional Craft is primarily focussed on activities concerned with workshop on textile designing and block printing on fabric and paper.Himalayan Traditional craft offers Cushion covers, Bed covers, Table cloths, Napkins, Place mats, Dhaka shawls, Scarves, Jackets, Sweaters, Gloves, Nepali paper and more.

Great handicratfs with no compromises

We hold our handicrafts to the highest standards and by purchasing our products, you make a difference in the lives of low income producers in Nepal.
